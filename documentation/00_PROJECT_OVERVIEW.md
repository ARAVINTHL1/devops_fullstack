## Abstract

The rapid growth of digital technology has transformed traditional business operations into smart and efficient systems. Small-scale jewellery businesses often rely on manual processes for managing inventory, sales, and employee activities, which can lead to inefficiencies, data errors, and lack of proper tracking.

This project presents the development of a Mobile Application for Dharani Jewellery Management System, designed specifically for shop owners and workers. Initially developed as a web-based application using ReactJS, Node.js, and MongoDB, it was later converted into a mobile application to improve accessibility and usability.

Unlike customer-based e-commerce applications, this system is designed exclusively for internal use by jewellery shop staff with:
- Secure login system for authorized users only
- Product management and stock tracking
- Sales recording and monitoring
- Employee operations
- Real-time data synchronization

**Key Benefits:**
- Improves business productivity
- Ensures accurate record management
- Supports digital transformation in small jewellery businesses
- Provides simple, secure, and efficient mobile-based solution
